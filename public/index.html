North Foxbridge Railways

Position: Development Manager

North Foxbridge Railways is a new game that I have been working on for the platform Roblox. I currently hold the position of Development Manager alongside 1 other co-worker. I oversee the entire development process of the game and manage a team of 12 developers working on different projects for the community. As a Development Manager my job also includes meeting with the different departments giving them a brief overview of the changes that have been made to the game in the past update so they can inform the supervisors. I do not have the direct community based role; it is more of a behind the scenes working with the teams that bring the experience to life. On top of this I also do most of the scripting for the game's core system such as scripting all the functions for the players GUI, scripting the movement systems for the trains, ect. I have been in this position since the game was founded and through my time I have learned many new things about myself and about the team I work with.
